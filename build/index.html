W Allan & Sons

Scrap Metal Merchants, Carlisle

Established for Over 70 Years

W Allan & Sons is a family run scrap metal merchant established for over 70 years. We pride ourselves in providing an efficient and reliable service to both new and existing customers. We are licenced from the environment agency and the city council to meet your needs, small or large.

Ferrous & Non-Ferrous Metals

We deal in ferrous (steel cast etc.) and non-ferrous (copper, brass, aluminium etc.) metals, purchased at a fair price.

Skips for Hire

We also provide skips from 10 cubic yards to 40 cubic yards to hire. Please contact us for further information.
